> 这段代码是从网络上找的，最后会生成3D的红心，我觉得很有意思，故分享一下。

```
% volume data
step = 0.05;
[X,Y,Z] = meshgrid(-3:step:3, -3:step:3, -3:step:3);
F = (-(X.^2).*(Z.^3)-(9/80).*(Y.^2).*(Z.^3))+((X.^2)+(9/4).*(Y.^2)+(Z.^2)-1).^3;
 
% shaded surface
isosurface(X,Y,Z,F,0)
lighting phong
axis equal
view(-39,30)
set(gcf, 'Color','w')
colormap flag
axis off
```
